Rules & Terms

Preamble

ArdaCraft strives to provide its community with the foundations for the most enjoyable and best creative experience possible. It is the community itself that will ultimately help us realise that potential. We ask all members of our community to conduct themselves according to our one simple rule:

The golden rule

Don't act in a way that is detrimental towards the server - This includes, but is not limited to, acts of: harassment, griefing and trolling. The interpretation of this rule is at the discretion of ArdaCraft's moderators.

Expanding on the rules

Some questions and things to consider

The Environment

Players are allowed to joke around, and the enviornment is not too serious. Don't get offended by a simple joke.

Griefing

Griefing is not tolerated and players who grief will lose their rank accordingly, along with other punishments deemed necessary.

Punishment

Punishment will be deterimined on a case by case basis; punishments are made at the discretion of the admins.

Who to contact

If you have any problems with the rules, you should contact Fornad, Freshmilkymilk, or any of the moderators on the forums.
